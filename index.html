About Me

I am a developer based in Durham, NC with a background in business and finance. After working at a bank for five years, I decided to make a drastic career change and learn about computer programming. Prior to the Iron Yard I took a few community college courses in Java. I also have experience developing using the Agile process and Test Driven Development.
